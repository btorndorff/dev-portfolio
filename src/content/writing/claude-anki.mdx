I started learning Vietnamese again back in the fall after a few year detour on becoming conversational in Japanese. Language learning has always been teeth gritting work for me especially the beginning where like a baby you know next to nothing when it comes to vocab and grammar. I had these expectations going in but I did not expect how much worse the resources were for vietnamese compared to japanese, none of the fancy apps and chrome extenstions let alone a proper 1000 most common words Anki deck which I assumed was nicely made for most languages already.

the 2 big issues:

- no proper anki decks for Vietnamese
- general issue with anki where its a headache to understand and personalize (IMO)

claude anki solves this by abstrcting understanding of how anki works and makes it easy to have the grow deck with you and your needs
